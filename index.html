About Us

Pong Game is a classic arcade game that simulates table tennis. Players control paddles and try to hit the ball past the opponent's paddle. It's a simple yet addictive game that tests your reflexes and strategy.

Key Features:

  • Single-player and two-player modes
  • Adjustable ball and text size
  • Realistic physics simulation

Experience the nostalgia of the retro arcade era with Pong Game!
